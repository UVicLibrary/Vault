Error 431: Request Headers Too Large

To resolve this error, you need to clear your cookies for this specific domain. Instructions with screenshots are available here, with text instructions for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari below.

If your browser is not included, you may need to search for instructions on how to clear cookies/cache for a specific website.

Instructions for Chrome

  1. Select Menu > Settings > Privacy and Security > Third-party cookies or Cookies and site data (depending on Chrome version).
  2. Select See all cookies and site data and search for uvic.ca. Click to expand the dropdown and click the trash icon for vault.library.uvic.ca.

Instructions for Edge

  1. Select Settings and more ... > Settings > Cookies and site permissions.
  2. Under Cookies and data stored, select Manage and delete cookies and site data > See all cookies and site data. Search for vault.library.uvic.ca.
  3. Select the down arrow to the right of the site and select Delete.

Instructions for Firefox

  1. Click the three horizontal lines to open the menu > Privacy & Security.
  2. Under Cookies and Site Data, select Manage Data.
  3. Search for uvic.ca. Select uvic.ca and click Remove Selected.

Instructions for Safari

  1. Go to Safari > Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
  2. Choose the website and select Remove.
